Nigeria

Nigeria is a country located in West Africa. It is the most populous country on the continent and is known for its diverse cultures, languages, and ethnic groups. Nigeria's capital is Abuja, while its largest city is Lagos, which is a major economic and cultural hub in Africa. The country is rich in natural resources, particularly oil and gas, which play a significant role in its economy. Nigeria has a variety of landscapes, including savannas, rainforests, and coastal plains along the Gulf of Guinea. The official language is English, but there are over 500 indigenous languages spoken throughout the country, including Yoruba, Igbo, and Hausa
